At Bikolos Nur Academy, learning is planned to be a valuable lifelong asset. Built on modern thinking methods (Thinking Maps), we help children build logical skills, high ethical values, and moral discipline.

Why We Are Different

Teaching Minds How to Understand

We use structured visual maps so kids can analyze math, science, and grammar naturally instead of just listening and writing notes.

Thinking Maps

Using eight special brain visual maps, we show kids how to categorize, compare, and break down tough ideas by themselves.

Active Classrooms

Our classrooms are fully student-centered. Every child participates, speaks up, and learns to work nicely in groups.

Strong Moral Character

Integrating Islamic principles (Akhlaq and Tarbiya) so kids grow up to be honest, respectful, and polite citizens.
